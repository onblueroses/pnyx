The Erscheinung Model

Who is speaking?

Theory

Arendt: the Erscheinungsraum requires a who behind the what. AI-generated text has no who. So we detect the absence.

Therefore

Fine-tuned DeBERTa + 85 heuristic signals. Sub-millisecond. On-device via ONNX. When there's no who, the content fades — not blocks.

  • [1:00-1:15]
  • Erscheinungsraum: requires a WHO behind the WHAT
  • AI text simulates speech without a speaker - discloses what, never who
  • Two tiers: 85 heuristic patterns (PNAS Biber, PubMed vocab, Pangram DAMAGE paper) + fine-tuned DeBERTa
  • 8 hand-crafted features: TTR, hapax rate, sentence variance, bigram uniqueness, contractions...
  • Sub-millisecond heuristics, on-device via ONNX
  • Fades, not blocks. Fog on glass, not a wall. Click to reveal
  • Presence matters. Absence should be visible, not censored

The Habermas Model

What is being said?

Theory

Habermas: every speech act raises validity claims. Wahrheit — is a truth claim being made? Richtigkeit — is there argument structure? We built a head for each.

Therefore

Two binary classification heads on DeBERTa-v3-small. One head per validity dimension. 10K samples. F1 0.974. ONNX, on-device, sub-second.

  • [1:15-1:30]
  • Habermas: 3 validity claims - Wahrheit (truth), Richtigkeit (rightness), Wahrhaftigkeit (sincerity)
  • We operationalize the first two. One binary head each. One forward pass
  • DeBERTa-v3-small, fine-tuned from NLI checkpoint (already understands entailment)
  • 12,500 training samples, balanced across all 4 label cells
  • F1 0.974. Focal loss + label smoothing to reduce overconfidence
  • 271 MB FP16 ONNX, sub-second in-browser via Web Worker
  • Theory gave us two validity dimensions. We built exactly two heads

Explore Mode

How should we disagree?

Theory

Mouffe: consensus is not always the goal. Disagreement is a democratic resource. Transform antagonism into agonism.

Therefore

Three rounds on any claim. Three moves: stance, concession, escalation. No move is ranked above another.

  • [1:30-1:45]
  • Mouffe: transform antagonism (enemy) into agonism (adversary)
  • Explore on any claim. 3 rounds, 3 moves per round
  • Stance: deepens position - what else does this commit you to?
  • Concession: agonistic move - acknowledge other side has ground. Should cost something
  • Escalation: follow logic further than comfort allows
  • No move ranked above another
  • System prompts encode all 3 theorists. Each round shifts validity dimensions
