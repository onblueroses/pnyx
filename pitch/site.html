Architecture

Four layers of discourse visibility

Each layer is grounded in political theory. Each theorist maps to a feature. The theory didn't just inspire the architecture - it determined it.

Detect
Is there a real person behind this text?

The Erscheinung model checks for genuine human presence. AI-generated text simulates speech without a speaker - it discloses a what but never a who. When there's no who, the content fades like fog on glass.

Arendt - Erscheinungsraum
See
What claims are being made?

The Habermas model extracts validity claims from every post. Wahrheit: are factual assertions present? Richtigkeit: is there argument structure? The system makes the structure of the speech act visible without judging the content.

Habermas - Validity Claims
Pause
Which claim are you responding to?

When you click reply, Pnyx surfaces the extracted claims and asks: which one are you engaging with? Three seconds of friction between impulse and reaction. Not a gate - an orientation moment.

Friction Design - 40% behavior shift (Twitter 2021)
Explore
Three rounds of structured disagreement

Click Explore on any claim for branching deliberation. Three moves per round: stance, concession, escalation. No move is ranked above another. The point isn't agreement - it's mutual intelligibility.

Principles

Three commitments

Sovereignty

Democratic infrastructure that depends on nobody's permission. No server, no API, no terms of service. 457 MB of models running on your device. Even if we disappeared, Pnyx keeps working.

Agonism

The other position has a right to exist. We don't engineer consensus. We don't score disagreement as failure. Conflict is a democratic resource - Mouffe's insight made operational.

Discourse

The structure of public speech should be legible to everyone. Not judged, not moderated - legible. What claims are being made? Is there reasoning? Who listened? Habermas made visible.

Get Started

Install Pnyx

Chrome extension. No account. No server. Works on LinkedIn and X.

Quick start

  1. 1. Download the extension (.zip) from the link below
  2. 2. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
  3. 3. Enable "Developer mode" (top right toggle)
  4. 4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the unzipped folder
  5. 5. Navigate to LinkedIn or X - Pnyx loads automatically

Models load on first visit (~5s). After that, scoring is instant. For Explore mode, add an OpenRouter API key in the extension settings - all other features work without any key.
